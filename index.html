The Targets

Everyone can play a part in achieving the Global Goals. These ten targets can guide us to take action and ensure quality education for all.

Free Primary & Secondary Education

Ensure every child has access to free, quality schooling.

Early Childhood Learning

Support programs that give young children the best start in life.

Affordable Higher Education

Create opportunities for all to access universities, colleges, and skills training.

Skills for Work

Promote practical and technical training that prepares people for good jobs.

Scholarships and Support

Expand financial help for students in developing countries.

Equal Access for All

Remove barriers so girls, boys, and disadvantaged groups can learn equally.
